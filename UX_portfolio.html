Tufts JumboCode: Ocean Alliance Team

Before our semester ended abruptly due to the Covid-19 pandemic, my team, Ocean Alliance, and I were working on this incredible project. I was the leader of the FrontEnd team, and we were working hard to make these wire frames come to life.

JumboCode is an organization at Tufts that helps local non-profits with free programming services. My team and I were helping Ocean Alliance Whale Conservation to build a program that identified whale types from whale footage by using machine learning to distinguish between blowholes. After identifying which kind of whale a certain whale in uploaded footage is, we would store these videos in a sorted gallery.

My team and I used HTML, JavaScript, CSS, jQuery, and Bootstrap to build our interface. Although we were unable to finish, I was and still am extremely passionate about this project. I learned how to work with other teams like the BackEnd and Machine Learning teams towards a common goal.
